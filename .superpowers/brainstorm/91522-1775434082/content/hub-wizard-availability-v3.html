Design Summary

  • 4 term type tabs: Fall, Spring, Summer, Winter
  • Fall & Spring: Full hourly availability grid (same as before)
  • Summer & Winter: Simple yes/no toggle — "Is this instructor available to teach?"
  • "Copy Fall → Spring" quick action since these are often identical
  • Status cards show all four at a glance with appropriate summaries
  • Pre-population: When a new term is created, availability auto-populates from the matching term-type template
