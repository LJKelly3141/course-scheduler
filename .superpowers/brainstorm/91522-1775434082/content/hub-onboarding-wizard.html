Wizard Flow

  • Step 1 — Profile: Required fields (name, email, department, type) highlighted with blue borders. Everything else optional. Covers contact + employment info in one form.
  • Step 2 — Scheduling Preferences: Modality constraint as visual cards (easier than a dropdown for 4 options). Optional initial note. Can skip to save immediately.
  • Step 3 — Availability: Same grid as the Availability tab. Defaults to all-available. "Copy from existing instructor" for quick setup. Can skip — availability can always be set later.
  • "Skip & Save" on steps 2 and 3 — only step 1 (profile) is required. Respects that you might onboard quickly and fill in details later.
  • Opens as overlay/modal over the hub — after saving, the new instructor appears selected in the roster.
