Capabilities

Everything a department chair
actually needs

Built by a chair, for chairs. No enterprise sales calls, no per-seat pricing, no features you'll never use.

Conflict Detection

Room double-bookings, instructor overlaps, and capacity violations caught instantly. Hard conflicts block finalization; soft warnings stay advisory.

Visual Schedule Grid

Interactive Mon–Fri timetable with drag-and-drop. View by room, instructor, or course level. Color-coded sections make patterns visible at a glance.

Enrollment Analytics

Multi-year trend analysis, enrollment forecasting, and room utilization reports. Identify growing and declining courses before they become problems.

Smart Import

Import existing schedules from XLSX with fuzzy instructor matching. Preview changes before committing. Export to XLSX, HTML, or push directly to a web page.

Instructor Management

Availability grids, workload tracking with credit overload warnings, modality preferences, and ICS calendar export to send schedules directly to instructors.

Course Rotation

Plan multi-year course rotations to ensure required courses are offered regularly. Track which courses are due and which are overdue across academic years.

Workflow

From blank slate to
published schedule

Three steps. No training manual required.

Import your data

Drop in your rooms, instructors, and courses from an existing XLSX. Or enter them manually. The app handles fuzzy matching and deduplication.

Build the schedule

Drag sections onto the timetable grid. The conflict engine validates every move in real time. Use suggestions to find open rooms and time slots automatically.

Finalize and share

Lock the term when it's clean. Export to XLSX for the registrar, email ICS calendar files to instructors, or print a room-by-room view for the hallway.
